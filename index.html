About Me

Hi, my name is Thom, but in the FPV world, I go by the nickname Kwartz. Based in France, I started flying FPV drones in 2023 with some cinematic. Today I specialize in mountain surfing as well as freestyle styles, and I absolutely love creating videos with various styles and music. You can check out my projects on YouTube, where I showcase my latest creations. My motto is "Space is our limit", which reflects my passion and approach to FPV flying. Fell free to contact me in the section contact of the website or in instagram. Enjoy you flight.

Drones

7inch

One of my favorite drones is the Jeno 7-inch. The Jeno is an open-source French frame project that offers various frame options. I use T-Motor F90 1500KV motors. For the electronics, I fly with the foxeer f7 v2 and flycolor 65a, a DJI o3 with two TrueRC core antenna, a Radiomaster Diversity RP4TD ELRS 2.4G, a Foxeer M10 for GPS, and a Vifly Finder 2 for the buzzer. For the propellers, I use Gemfan 7040-3. And the best liions voltora sun and moon series

5inch

This is my most versatile drone, but I primarily use it for freestyle. Equipped with Pacer V3 1950KV 2207 motors, a JeNo 5.1", caddx vista, Ethix P4 props, and speedybee f405, and with ovonic 1100mah lipo this drone is ideal for freestyle beginners like me.

Other

Of course, I don’t just have drones; I need other equipment to fly. I use the dji V2 goggles. For maintaining a good connection, I use the Radiomaster TX15max red edition with ELRS 2.4. Recently, I’ve started using the Hota D6 Pro for charging my batteries,for editing i use davinci resolve and for filming, I use a the DJI action 2 for his crazy FOV. Cap Higuer, a stunning hike on the Spanish side connecting Hendaye to Pasaia, offers beautiful weather and landscapes in shades of blue and green. I managed only three flights with my 7-inch drone, but the footage remains amazing. If you get the chance to go, don’t hesitate.
